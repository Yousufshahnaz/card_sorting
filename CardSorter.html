Thank you for taking part in this activity as it will help us organise the new intranet based on YOU.

Please drag and drop the items from the yellow 'box' on the left into any of the 'boxes' on the right (You can also organise the items under other items that you have already moved, and change the name of the boxes: just click on the names you want to change to edit them.)

If you would not know where to place an item, please put it under 'Search', since that is what we would expect you to do if you cannot reach an item via the menus.

There is no right or wrong answer. Just do what comes naturally.

When you're done click the 'Submit' button at bottom of the page.
